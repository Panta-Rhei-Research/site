# Empirical Observability and Measurement

## Requirement

The kernel must recover the conditions under which physical quantities become observable and measurable.

## What This Target Requires

- Measurement events, calibration procedures, repeatability, stable records, and relation between formal quantities and empirical records.

This v0.1 item fixes the public burden of proof. Detailed formal, empirical, or bridge verification remains routed through the Verify and Results lanes.
