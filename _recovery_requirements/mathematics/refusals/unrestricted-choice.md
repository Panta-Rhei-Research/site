# Unrestricted Choice

## Requirement

Unrestricted forms of choice are not silently available as ambient machinery.

## What This Target Requires

- The kernel must distinguish primitive ontology, object-language representation, constructive recovery, bridge transfer, and external classical proof.

## What Is Not Refused

- choice-like principles as object theories
- bridge use under explicit assumptions

## Related Recovery Targets

- MREC-M4
- MREC-M5
