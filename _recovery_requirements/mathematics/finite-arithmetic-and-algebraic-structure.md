# Finite Arithmetic and Algebraic Structure

## Requirement

The kernel must recover finite arithmetic and algebraic calculation without silently importing completed classical infinity.

## What This Target Requires

- Finite numerals, successor/progression behavior, and finite calculation.
- Ring-like and field-like structures where required downstream.
- Explicit boundary behavior for classical completed infinity claims.
