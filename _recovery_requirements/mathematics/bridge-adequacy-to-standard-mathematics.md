# Bridge Adequacy to Standard Mathematics

## Requirement

The kernel must state which standard structures can be transferred, reconstructed, represented, or re-grounded, and under what assumptions.

## What This Target Requires

- Explicit bridge criteria for recovered structures and standard mathematical counterparts.
- Clear marking of preserved axioms, failed assumptions, transferable theorems, and non-meaningful transfers.
