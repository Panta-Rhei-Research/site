## Conclusion

The three remediation waves achieved their goals:

1. **Framework modules**: 13 → 24.2 (+11.1). The site's #1 quality gap is closed. All 10 sampled modules are Frontier tier.
2. **Below-threshold pages**: 13 → 1. Only `no-hawking-radiation` remains below the 14-point floor.
3. **NC dimension**: The weakest dimension (3.36) is now among the strongest (4.8) on remediated pages.
4. **J2 Skeptic Audit**: Upgraded from 4/5 to 5/5. All five journeys now score 4 or 5.

The site's quality profile has shifted from a two-tier structure (strong editorial pages + weak data-driven pages) toward a consistently high baseline. The editorial pages that were already Frontier have been confirmed or lifted by 1-2 points through cross-linking. The framework modules that were the site's Achilles heel are now among its strongest pages.

**Estimated site-wide mean CQNF**: ~20.5 (up from 18.1), approaching the release target of 19.5+.
