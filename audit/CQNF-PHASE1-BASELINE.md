## Key Findings

### 1. Trust Language is the site's signature strength (TL mean 4.67)
- 25 of 43 pages score TL=5
- Only 2 pages score TL=3 (Publications root, Impressum — structural, not editorial)
- The program's epistemic discipline is the strongest differentiator

### 2. Narrative Coherence is the weakest dimension (NC mean 3.79)
- Systematic pattern: most pages lack **in-text hyperlinks** to other pages
- Right-rail related pages provide navigation, but prose cross-references are rare
- The Research Program batch (NC mean 4.1) and Framework batch (NC mean 4.06) both show this — conceptual threading is good, but explicit in-text links are almost absent
- **Fix**: Adding 2-3 in-text cross-references per page would lift most NC scores from 3/4 to 4/5

### 3. Five pages need remediation (below site target of 19)

| Page | CQNF | Primary Issue |
|------|:----:|---------------|
| Research Notes index | 16 | Only 77 words of body prose (PS=2) |
| Publications root | 17 | Only ~130 words (PS=3), reads as catalog |
| Impressum | 17 | Structurally limited by page type (NC=2 expected) |
| What Framework Does Not Assume | 17 | Only 276 words (PS=3), needs development |
| Impact root | 18 | Only ~90 words (PS=3), thin for a lane root |

### 4. The Research Program / About sequence is world-class (mean 23.5)
- 10/10 pages at Frontier tier
- Historical Context scored a perfect 25/25
- This is the editorial high-water mark of the site

### 5. Framework staircase has consistent quality but weak orientation (RO mean 3.25)
- 13 of 16 pages score RO=3 — no explicit step numbering or backward/forward page names
- Adding "This is step N of 16" and "Building on [previous page]..." would lift RO significantly
