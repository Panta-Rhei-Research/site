Chapter 2 previewed eight guarantees—one from each of the seven Clay Millennium
Problems and one from the Langlands programme—that local Hartogs bulk projections
require in order to glue into a single, globally coherent three-dimensional space.
At that stage, the preview was a *promise*: structural roles were identified,
but no theorem backed them.
Parts IV through VII have now earned every one.
This chapter revisits the gluing table with every cell carrying a theorem number,
introduces a naming scheme for the eight structural forces that the guarantees provide,
and answers the question posed in Chapter 1:
*Where does physics live?*
Physics lives at E₁,
and the eight forces are what E₁ delivers.
