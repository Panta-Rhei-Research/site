Part VI has proved three results at enrichment level $E_1$: the BSD Coherence Theorem (Theorem [thm:bsd-coherence-theorem], Ch. 47), the Functoriality Theorem (Theorem [thm:functoriality-theorem], Ch. 49), and the finite factorization pasting of the enriched bi-square (Theorem [thm:finite-factorization-pasting], Ch. 50). This chapter assembles the full enrichment tower $E_0 ⊂neq E_1 ⊂neq E_2$, placing Part VI's results in the context of the three-level scaling chain that runs from Book I through the present volume. Section 1 states the Enrichment Tower Assembly theorem (Theorem [thm:enrichment-tower-assembly]), which asserts that each enrichment level adds irreducible structure and that the three bi-squares—algebraic, enriched, computational—form a coherent scaling chain. Section 2 isolates what Part VI proves: BSD coherence, Langlands functoriality, and enriched finite factorization, with scope boundaries. Section 3 states the export contracts to Parts IX and X (Remark [rem:part-vi-export-contracts]). Section 4 previews Part IX's computational door and closes with the Part VI Coherence Summary (Remark [rem:part-vi-coherence-summary]).
