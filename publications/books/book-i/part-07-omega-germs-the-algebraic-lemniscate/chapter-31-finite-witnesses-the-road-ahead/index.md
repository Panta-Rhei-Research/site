The hinge block (Parts IV–VII)
has transformed Category $τ$
from a countable combinatorial object
into a structured mathematical universe
with coordinates, dimension, polarity,
omega-germs, and the algebraic lemniscate boundary.
This chapter reflects on the hinge block as a whole,
explains the local–global bridge
that connects finite computations to infinite algebraic structure,
and previews the remaining Parts of Book I.
