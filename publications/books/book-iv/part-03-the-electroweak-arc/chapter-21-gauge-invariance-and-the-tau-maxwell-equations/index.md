What IS electric charge? Not a mysterious property stuck to particles,
but **holonomy**—the phase accumulated when transporting around
loops in τ³. And what ARE Maxwell's equations? Not postulates, but
the **curvature equations** of the U(1) connection on the B-sector
of 𝕃. This chapter shows how gauge invariance emerges from the
lemniscate boundary 𝕃=S¹∨ S¹, identifies the AB boundary
as the interface between the electromagnetic B-sector (γ-generator)
and the weak A-sector (π-generator), then derives all four Maxwell
equations from the holomorphic structure of τ³.
