Social life is organized at multiple scales, from the intimate pair
to the planetary species. This chapter classifies nine structural
spheres — self, pair, household, friendship circle, community,
city, nation, civilization, and species — and analyses the
coordination failures that arise when problems at one scale must be
solved at another. The Nine Global Spheres Classification (VII.D79)
provides a structural taxonomy grounded in the social ontology of
Chapter [ch:objects-dignity-worlds].
