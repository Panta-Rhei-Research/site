The centrepiece of the ethics expansion. The categorical imperative is
not postulated but *derived*: it is the minimal j-closed fixed
point in the presheaf topos over the site of rational perspectives.
The CI structure — maxim space, universalization endofunctor,
coherence test, respect operator — forms a typed operator graph that
is stable under the dignity modality j_dig. Any weaker
structure fails j-closure; any stronger structure is redundant.
This gives uniqueness up to natural isomorphism and establishes
that the CI already lives in the admissible subworld.
