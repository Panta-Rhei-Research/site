The abstract 4+1 sector decomposition of
Chapter [ch:sector-decomposition] is now equipped with its
concrete operational machinery. Each sector receives a
*witness bundle* (the typed evidence that certifies membership),
a *vacuum* (the canonical minimiser of the sector's defect
functional), and a *normaliser* (the NF-coded bounded pipeline
that computes coherence verdicts). Two shadow families—the
VM/ZFC-PA shadow and the epistemic/pragmatic shadow—are introduced.
The Rigidity Corollary guarantees each sector's internal consistency.
The Shadow Soundness Lemma proves that each shadow is sound with
respect to its sector.
