The E₃ enrichment layer is formalized. At E₂, a living system
carries and decodes its own code (SelfDesc). At E₃, a system
*models the fact that it carries and decodes its own code*
(SelfDesc-of-SelfDesc). The MetaDecode operator takes the evaluator
itself as input. The E₃ Non-Emptiness Theorem is proved constructively
via the BH basin law-code as canonical carrier, establishing that the
metaphysics layer is inhabited.
