This chapter closes the empirical sector S_E of Book VII. Across
twenty-five chapters (Parts II and III, Chapters 16–40), the empirical
register has been treated exhaustively: what exists, what is true, what
can be known, how it is perceived, how it is experienced, how it is
shared, how it is embodied, and why the gap between being and knowing
is not a gap at all. This synthesis surveys the structural harvest,
records the full registry of S_E entries, connects the empirical
sector forward to the three remaining sectors (S_D, S_P, S_C),
and explains why S_E is complete—why the empirical register has
nothing further to deliver, and why what remains belongs to other
registers.
