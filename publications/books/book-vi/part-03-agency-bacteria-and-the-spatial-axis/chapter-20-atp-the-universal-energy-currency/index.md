ATP is the universal energy currency of life: its hydrolysis releases energy while ADP phosphorylation stores it, cycling billions of times per cell per day. ATP synthase is a rotary molecular machine that realizes Poincar\'e circulation at the nanometer scale, driven by the proton-motive force. The sector template's canonical uniqueness explains why life uses one energy currency rather than many.
