## One coherence kernel. Four layers. Seven books.

The **Panta Rhei Research Program** investigates whether one coherence-first, self-contained framework can support a full-stack model of reality — from mathematical foundations through physics and life to the terminal metaphysical layer where proof reaches the boundary of commitment.

The framework begins from five generators, seven axioms, and one progression operator. From this minimal kernel, it earns arithmetic, analysis, geometry, physics, biology, and philosophy as structural consequences — all compiled and verified by Lean's kernel with zero sorry in Books I–VI.

## The enrichment ladder

The program is organized by four enrichment layers:

- **E₀ — Mathematics** (Books I–III): The coherence kernel, holomorphic interior, and the structural hinge where physics becomes locatable
- **E₁ — Physics** (Books IV–V): The self-describing universe — microcosm and macrocosm from one constant
- **E₂ — Life** (Book VI): Life as self-decoding distinctions — a genuine structural enrichment
- **E₃ — Metaphysics** (Book VII): The final self-enrichment — where proof ends and commitment begins
