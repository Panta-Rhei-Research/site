## What this hinge must establish

This hinge must show that prime polarity and the Legendre classifier arise as structural polarity within the framework rather than as an external number-theoretic overlay.

## Why it belongs here

Primes become finite witnesses of boundary behavior. The polarity structure gives the kernel nontrivial spectral direction before later spectral algebra or physics claims are attempted.
