## What this step builds

The ninth step concerns formal systems as internal objects: ZFC-like theories, Lean-like kernels, the tau-kernel as represented object, proof as act, computation as performed process, and meta-language internalization.

## Why this step is required

The program begins with a formal kernel, but self-hosting is not available at the beginning. It requires reflective symbolic structure capable of writing, interpreting, manipulating, and reasoning over formal systems as objects.
