**Sector S_D (Diagrammatic), Part 1 of 3.**
What is beauty? **Pre-symbolic resonance** precedes language—aesthetics is primary,
not derivative. Beauty is invariance under transformation; elegance is minimal constraint
tension. The golden ratio, fractals, musical harmony, visual composition, and architectural
flow all manifest the same categorical structure. Style is preserved motif. The sublime
marks the boundary where comprehension fails yet elevation occurs. Environmental aesthetics
reveals nature's beauty as emergent invariance and ecological coherence.
Aesthetics opens the diagrammatic sector because its content is pre-linguistic:
pattern recognition before symbolic encoding.
