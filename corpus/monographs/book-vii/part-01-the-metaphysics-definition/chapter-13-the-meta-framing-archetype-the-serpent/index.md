The third archetype candidate. The meta-framing archetype formalizes
the structural pattern of reframing the frame itself: structure that
models its own framing operation. The serpent in Genesis 3 is the
structural prototype—it does not attack the content of the command
but reframes its context. Trickster figures across traditions (Loki,
Coyote, Hermes, Anansi) all exhibit the same pattern. The archetype
is morally neutral: meta-framing can serve enlightenment (Socratic
irony) or destruction (manipulation). What matters is register
discipline.
