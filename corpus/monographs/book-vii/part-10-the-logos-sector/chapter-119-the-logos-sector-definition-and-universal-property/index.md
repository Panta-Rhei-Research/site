Part IX completed the commitment sector S_C — mind as internal topos, consciousness as global section, the self-recognition loop. Part X opens at the structural consequence of that work: the Logos sector S_L, defined as S_D ∩ S_C, the unique location in the 4+1 sector decomposition of τ at E₃ where the diagrammatic register *Reg_D* and the commitment register *Reg_C* do not merely meet but coincide. A content φ is S_L-admissible if and only if its *Reg_D*-validity and *Reg_C*-stability are mutually witnessing — to prove it is already to commit to it, and to commit is already to have proved it. Chapter 119 gives S_L its formal definition (VII.D86) and proves it unique up to natural isomorphism (VII.T45). The name "Logos" is assigned entirely by universal property, in the same spirit that a categorical product is named by its universal property: the name describes the structure, nothing more.
