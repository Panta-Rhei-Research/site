This chapter performs for the practical sector what Chapter 75 performed for the diagrammatic sector: it catalogs the deliverables, reveals the internal architecture unifying them, and locates that architecture within the broader enrichment structure of τ. Nine deliverables are assembled: dignity as label-independence (VII.D65/T30); the CI as sheaf condition (VII.T31); the four ethical tests (VII.D69); moral monodromy as topological source of tragedy (VII.D68/T33); virtue as fixed point and flourishing as global section (VII.D70/T34); the CI proof programme through Stages K, S, and CI (VII.T36, VII.T37, VII.P21, VII.L13); the commitment register as fourth mode of reason (VII.D72/T38); the Kant-τ correspondence (VII.D73/R33); and the earned/unearned distinction with the pseudo-paradox dissolution (VII.D74/P22). The Kant's Heroic Restraint remark (VII.R34) shows that these nine deliverables form a closed arc: dignity (ground) → CI as sheaf condition (universalizability criterion) → four tests (decidable procedure) → CI proof programme (structural derivation) → Kant-τ correspondence (historical validation) → earned/unearned (methodological discipline). The Register-Enrichment Correspondence (VII.D75) provides the second structural result: Reg_P↔E₀ (pre-structural moral intuition), Reg_E↔E₁ (structured empirical data), Reg_D↔E₂ (formal proof and certification), Reg_C↔E₃ (saturation, where only commitment remains). This mapping preserves order (each register presupposes the preceding ones), explains why Reg_P maps to E₀ (moral intuition is pre-structural, not post-empirical), and reveals that the diagrammatic sector S_D and the practical sector S_P are both traversals of the same enrichment hierarchy with different starting content: patterns vs. obligations. The chapter closes by pointing to Part VIII, which extends the analysis from individual maxims to institutional norms, from dignity to justice, and from the CI to constitutional constraints.

## What this chapter contributes

- **Definitions / Axioms:** *VII.D75 — Register-Enrichment Correspondence* (τ-effective). Formal order-preserving mapping between the four registers and the four enrichment layers (Reg_P↔E₀, Reg_E↔E₁, Reg_D↔E₂, Reg_C↔E₃), establishing the structural unity of the diagrammatic and practical sectors.
- **Key results:** *VII.R34 — Kant's Heroic Restraint* (τ-effective): the nine Part VII deliverables form a closed arc from dignity through the CI to methodological discipline; the arc parallels the pattern–symbol–proof arc of S_D as two traversals of the enrichment hierarchy.
- **Dependencies:** All Part VII results VII.D65 through VII.P22; enrichment layers E₀–E₃; pattern–symbol–proof arc of the diagrammatic sector (Chapter 75).
