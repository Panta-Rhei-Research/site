The three sub-registers correspond to increasing levels of structural articulation: pattern detection (pre-linguistic, enrichment level E₀), symbolic representation (E₁–E₂), and formal proof (E₂–E₃). Each stage builds on the previous; the arc is hierarchical but cumulative. The question "What can I prove?" has been answered; Part VII opens with "What ought I to do?"
