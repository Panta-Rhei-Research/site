The formal machinery of Part I — two predicates, a loop class, a sector template, seven derived hallmarks, and three predictions by absence — operates on physical carriers. This chapter provides the physical substrate: the seven categorical forces as they manifest at enrichment level E₂ in living systems, and the τ³ domain correspondence that maps the manifold's three structural components to their biological counterparts. The seven categorical forces (Book III) find biological instantiations: Riemann governs energy quantisation, manifest as the discrete ATP energy quantum (ΔG ≈ 30.5 kJ/mol); Poincaré governs circulation, manifest as blood flow, sap transport, and cytoplasmic streaming; Hodge governs harmonic decomposition, manifest as morphogenesis and spatial patterning; BSD governs rank of rational points, with a conjectural connection to the 20-amino-acid / 64-codon structure of the genetic code; Yang–Mills governs gauge coupling, manifest as protein folding and enzymatic catalysis; Navier–Stokes governs fluid dynamics at all biological scales; P vs NP governs computational complexity, manifest as neural learning and immune recognition. Each sector has a characteristic force profile defined by the Force-Sector Matching. The τ³ domain correspondence maps τ¹ (base) to temporal rhythm — the circadian cycle, the cell cycle, the mitotic reset at the smash point ω; T² (fiber) to spatial structure — cell architecture along radial and circumferential axes; ℒ = S¹ ∨ S¹ (boundary) to the cell membrane — the first physical realisation of the lemniscate Distinction. The correspondence is not merely analogical: the cell membrane must have ℒ topology because τ-Distinction requires a clopen boundary into 2_τ, and the simplest such boundary on τ³ is ℒ. The chapter closes with the argument that life is thermodynamically necessary: self-maintaining configurations (Distinction carriers) are entropy-production maximisers, SelfDesc systems are thermodynamic attractors because they resist dissolution within their basin, and the rapid appearance of life on Earth within the first 10% of the planet's existence is consistent with the Life configuration being an attractor of the defect landscape rather than an improbable accident.
