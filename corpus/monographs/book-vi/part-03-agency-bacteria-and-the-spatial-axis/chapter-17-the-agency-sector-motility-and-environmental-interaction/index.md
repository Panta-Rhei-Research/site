The agency sector is formally defined as the π-base restricted to E₂ carriers, with spatial motility as the defining predicate. A carrier satisfies spatial motility when its distinction is maintained while moving through the π-orbit, and the central theorem establishes agency as the stability of distinction under spatial displacement. Bacteria serve as the biological archetype of this sector.
