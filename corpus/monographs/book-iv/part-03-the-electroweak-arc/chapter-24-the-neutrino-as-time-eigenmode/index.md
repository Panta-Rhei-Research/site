Near-masslessness follows because τ¹ ≅ S¹ is one-dimensional with minimal τ² footprint; mass arises only from τ¹–τ² coupling in the fibration τ³ = τ¹ ×_f τ², which is small because the fibration is only mildly twisted. Charge neutrality follows because U(1) holonomy lives on τ² and the neutrino is primarily τ¹. Three flavors follow from dim(τ³) = 3. Majorana character follows from τ¹ ≅ S¹ being self-dual: a four-step proof identifies C_τ with the polarity involution σ on 𝕃, so all zero-holonomy modes satisfy C_τψ = ±ψ. The ν/ν̄ label is a helicity label, not an internal quantum number, so lepton number is not conserved and neutrinoless double β-decay must occur.
