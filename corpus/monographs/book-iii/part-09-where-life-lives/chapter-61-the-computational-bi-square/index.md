The bi-square of Book I (I.T41) has appeared at three enrichment levels:
algebraic (E₀), topological (E₀ → E₁),
and enriched (E₁+).
This chapter constructs the fourth and final bi-square—the
*computational bi-square* at E₂—and proves the
τ-Admissibility Collapse: τ-P_adm
= τ-NP_adm.
The proof assembles constant-width Cook–Levin tableaux (Ch 55),
polynomial CRT refinement (Ch 57),
and the Product-Meet Collapse—the E₂ identity
between searching for a witness (∧) and constructing
the composite (×).
