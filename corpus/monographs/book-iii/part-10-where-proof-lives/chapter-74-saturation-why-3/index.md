The Saturation Theorem (*III.T03*, Chapter 7) established E₄ = E₃ from the orbit structure of the coherence kernel, but that proof was abstract: it preceded the concrete content that Parts II–IX have since deposited at each enrichment level. This chapter revisits saturation with all of that content in hand and conducts three independent domain tests. Proof theory about proof theory is still proof theory: the self-modelling operator applied to a proof-theorist produces another proof-theorist who uses the same cut-elimination and conservativity tools. Meta-ZFC is still proof theory: the tower of formal systems (ZFC, ZFC + large cardinals, ...) grows vertically within E₂, and the single horizontal step that studies the entire tower is one E₃ act, not the beginning of an E₄. A hypothetical meta-Book III would operate in the same diagrammatic sector as Book III itself, the ω-coupling sector of E₃. In each case the self-modelling operator S satisfies S ∘ S = S: iterated self-modelling produces nothing new. The chapter crystallises this into two formal results and closes with a philosophical coda characterising the four enrichment levels as four irreducible modes of structure — mathematics, physics, computation, metaphysics — and arguing that the absence of E₄ is structural completeness rather than failure of imagination.

## What this chapter contributes

*III.T49* (Applied Saturation) is the chapter's principal theorem: the enrichment ladder E₀→E₁→E₂→E₃→E₃ is complete. Each of the first three arrows produces irreducible new structure; the fourth arrow is the identity. The proof applies the abstract Saturation Theorem (*III.T03*) to the concrete E₃ content of Chapters 72–73 and verifies domain-specifically that the self-modelling operator is idempotent in all three tested domains. *III.P31* (Terminal Level Characterization) captures the criterion intrinsically: an enrichment level E_k is terminal if and only if S_k ∘ S_k = S_k, equivalently every E_k-observer's self-model is already an E_k-object. The chapter proves that only E₃ satisfies this condition — at E₀, E₁, and E₂ the self-modelling operator is non-idempotent, producing the next rung. The philosophical coda is *metaphorical* in scope: it articulates why four modes exhaust structure, but it is interpretive synthesis rather than a formalisable proposition.

Applied Saturation is the penultimate structural theorem before the final synthesis. Chapter 75 (Architecture of Reality) uses *III.T49* alongside the Hinge Theorem (*III.T41*), No Knobs (*III.T42*), the Honest Claim (*III.T47*), and the Complete Dependency Chain (*III.D66*) to present the seven-book series as a single commutative diagram with one constant, zero free parameters, and four irreducible enrichment levels.
