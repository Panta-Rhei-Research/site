Part VI has proved three results at enrichment level E₁: the BSD Coherence Theorem (the relevant theorem, Ch. 47), the Functoriality Theorem (the relevant theorem, Ch. 49), and the finite factorization pasting of the enriched bi-square (the relevant theorem, Ch. 50). This chapter assembles the full enrichment tower E₀ ⊂neq E₁ ⊂neq E₂, placing Part VI's results in the context of the three-level scaling chain that runs from Book I through the present volume. Section 1 states the Enrichment Tower Assembly theorem , which asserts that each enrichment level adds irreducible structure and that the three bi-squares—algebraic, enriched, computational—form a coherent scaling chain. Section 2 isolates what Part VI proves: BSD coherence, Langlands functoriality, and enriched finite factorization, with scope boundaries. Section 3 states the export contracts to Parts IX and X (Remark [rem:part-vi-export-contracts]). Section 4 previews Part IX's computational door and closes with the Part VI Coherence Summary (Remark [rem:part-vi-coherence-summary]).
