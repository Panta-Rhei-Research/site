Chapters 62 and 63 mapped the structural source of all differences and confirmed that seventeen constructions survive the fork intact or in parallel form. This chapter addresses the hardest part of the audit: what does Category τ structurally refuse? These are not accidental omissions. Each of the sixteen Mode C constructions is blocked by a specific axiom or theorem, and the sixteen together form a coherent architecture — three interlocking barriers that protect the Mode D gains catalogued in Chapter 65.

## What this chapter contributes

The **Mode C Catalog** (*established*, *II.D91*) lists sixteen constructions that τ's axioms structurally block, grouped by their source. Axiom K6 (Object Closure) seals the universe at ℵ₀, forcing refusal of uncountable sets (refusal 1) and unrestricted comprehension (refusal 4); the cost is real — τ cannot construct ℝ, ℓ² Hilbert spaces, or Lebesgue measure — but the gain is categoricity (*I.T08*). Axiom K5 (Diagonal Discipline) limits iterated self-application to four levels, blocking the Cantor diagonal argument (refusal 3) and hyperoperators above tetration (refusal 11); the protection is paradox avoidance and safe self-enrichment. The forced sign j² = +1 (*I.T10*) converts the Cauchy–Riemann equations from elliptic to hyperbolic, blocking conformal maps (refusal 5), the Cauchy integral formula (refusal 14), and Riemann surfaces (refusal 15); the gain is the bipolar decomposition and the Central Theorem.

The case study is the **dimensional ladder collapse** of Several Complex Variables. Orthodox SCV exhibits qualitatively new holomorphic phenomena at each dimension — complete boundary determination at ℂ¹, Hartogs extension at ℂ², saturation at ℂ³ — driven by the **Archimedean-Elliptic Engine** (*established*, *II.D75*): Archimedean density combined with elliptic regularity. The engine is absent in τ. The **Simultaneous Rung Theorem** (*established*, *II.T47*) proves that τ³ exhibits all three dimensional features at once — complete boundary determination, a distinguished boundary (the lemniscate 𝕃 = S¹ ∨ S¹), and full Hartogs extension — without any of the pathologies (removable singularities, Cousin problems, Levi problem) associated with individual rungs in the orthodox ladder. The **Fourth Quadrant Ladder Collapse theorem** (*established*, *II.T48*) establishes why: without Archimedean density there are no scales between stages, without elliptic regularity there are no convergent power series to distinguish dimensions, and the wave equation's characteristics propagate in all coordinate dimensions simultaneously. **Categoricity implies no ladder** (*established*, *II.R31*): if the holomorphic function algebra is uniquely determined, the framework cannot exhibit different behaviors at different dimensions, and the ladder requires exactly that variability.

The sixteen refusals are not a menu. The **Protection Architecture** (*established*, *II.R38*) shows they form three interlocking barriers — K6, K5, and j² = +1 — each protecting a corresponding class of Mode D gains. Remove any one barrier and the corresponding gains fail. The costs are all-or-nothing consequences of the foundational commitments.

With the costs recorded honestly, Chapter 65 catalogues what τ gains in return: 23 theorems and properties that are provably impossible, independent, or false in ZFC/PA. The Protection Architecture of this chapter explains why each gain is available: it is the Mode D gain that the corresponding Mode C barrier protects.
