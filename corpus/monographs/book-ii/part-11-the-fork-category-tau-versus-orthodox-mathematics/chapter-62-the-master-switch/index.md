Before the five comparison modes of Chapter 61 can be applied construction by construction, there is a prior question: where do all the differences come from? This chapter answers it. Every structural divergence between Category τ and orthodox mathematics traces, through at most three intermediate steps, to a single algebraic fact: the split-complex unit satisfies j² = +1 while the orthodox imaginary unit satisfies i² = −1. That sign is not a design decision — it is forced by Prime Polarity (*I.T05* → *I.T10*). This chapter traces the cascade that flows from it.

## What this chapter contributes

The chapter's primary formal output is a three-part classification of the downstream consequences. The **Structural Sign Classification** (*established*, *II.D68*) assigns to each of twelve levels of mathematical structure a level-ℓ sign trade-off (ℰ_ℓ, ℋ_ℓ): the orthodox outcome versus the τ outcome. The classification satisfies completeness (every documented structural difference appears in some level), traceability (no trade-off requires more than three steps from the sign equation), and monotonicity (higher levels depend on lower ones). The **Rewiring Thesis** (*established*, *II.R23*) is the verifiable structural claim that the classification makes precise: it is falsifiable in principle — exhibit a structural difference requiring four or more steps — and no such counterexample has been found across Books I–II.

The twelve levels span scalar algebra (j² = +1 produces zero divisors and bipolar idempotents e± where i² = −1 gives a field), through PDE type (wave equation □f = 0 versus Laplace equation Δf = 0), propagation character, topology (Stone space versus Hausdorff manifold), cardinality (unique ω versus the ℵ-hierarchy), and the spectrum at infinity (ω-germs present versus absent). The **PDE Type Classification** (*established*, *II.D70*) isolates the analytic fulcrum: the single sign flip in the second Cauchy–Riemann equation converts the Laplacian into the d'Alembertian. The **Asymmetric Determination theorem** (*established*, *II.T44*) establishes that in τ-holomorphy, boundary data propagates directionally into the interior along wave characteristics — Hartogs extension, which requires deep elliptic regularity in classical analysis, becomes constructive wave-front propagation via CRT. The comprehensive **Rewiring Table** (*established*, *II.D72*) places all twelve levels side by side, with every entry backed by a theorem or registered definition.

The **Honest Accounting remark** (*established*, *II.R27*) names what each world pays. Orthodox mathematics buys algebraic closure, conformal geometry, ε-δ local analysis, the Laplacian's mean-value property, and Hilbert-space spectral theory; it pays with multiple infinities, non-constructive existence, imported topology, and non-categoricity. Category τ buys canonical ω, bipolar structure, constructive witnesses, and categoricity; it pays with no ε-δ, no local smoothness, no Laplacian, zero divisors, and no conformal maps. Neither list is longer because one side is better.
