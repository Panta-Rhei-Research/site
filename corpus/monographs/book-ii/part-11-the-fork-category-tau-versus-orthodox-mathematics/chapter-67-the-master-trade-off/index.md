Chapters –
developed the five modes systematically:
the master switch (Ch. ),
what survives (Ch. ),
what is refused (Ch. ),
what is gained (Ch. ),
and what is earned (Ch. ).

This chapter assembles the master trade-off:
the **combined gain–cost ledger**
across all five modes,
the **structural incompatibility theorem**
that proves the fork is not optional,
the **five thematic patterns**
that organize the differences,
the **physics mismatch**
that extends the fork to the physical world,
and the **Trade-Off Atomicity theorem**
that shows you cannot cherry-pick.
