This chapter is a **bridge**.
It summarizes what Books I and II have accomplished,
articulates the precise structural transition
from E₀⁽0⁾ to E₀⁽1⁾,
and declares—honestly, without claiming more
than has been earned—what Book III must do.
The enrichment ladder
E₀ → E₁ → E₂ → E₃
(I.D82, Book I)
is the organizing principle
for the entire Panta Rhei series.
At E₀⁽0⁾, Category T is a category
with holomorphic structure on its boundary.
At E₀⁽1⁾, T is a **self-enriched** category
with holomorphic structure extending to the full fibered product
τ³ = τ¹ ×_f T²,
the Central Theorem establishing
the holographic correspondence
O(τ³) ≅ A_spec(𝕃).
**the relevant definition (II.D58)
formalizes this transition
by listing its inputs and outputs.
**Remark [rem:enrichment-ladder**] (II.R16)
previews the full four-layer ladder
and identifies the honest limitations
at each transition.
Book III must climb from E₀⁽1⁾ to E₀⁽2⁾:
the physics layer,
where the eight spectral forces operate
in split-complex H_τ,
and the Millennium Problems
appear as spectral force manifestations.
That ascent is *declared*, not yet earned.
