## Open Corpus editions

Corpus Monographs are the open reading projections of the seven book-length research monographs. They carry the Book → Part → Chapter exposition inside the Corpus lane, where the constructed research body lives.

The citable release artifacts, DOI metadata, covers, and retail/download status remain in [Research Monographs]({{ '/publications/research-monographs/' | relative_url }}) under Publications.

<div class="notice note"><strong>Projection boundary.</strong> These pages are not a separate publication category. They are Corpus projections of the same monograph content, exposed here so readers can follow construction, dependencies, and related verification surfaces without treating Publications as the research body itself.</div>
