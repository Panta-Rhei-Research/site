We introduce ρ, the sole ontic operator of Category τ.
The operator ρ acts as a progression (successor) on each generator,
unfolding the infinite orbit rays that populate the universe.
Two kernel axioms govern its behaviour:
K2 declares ω the unique fixed point of ρ,
and K5 asserts that ω is unreachable —
no finite iteration of ρ on any other generator ever reaches ω.
Together, these axioms establish the *beacon*:
a fixed horizon that bounds the universe from above without belonging
to any orbit ray.
