The CRT isomorphism
ℤ_τ ≅ ∏_p ℤ_p

decomposes the boundary ring into local factors,
and the spectral characters χ_+, χ_-
(see Part X, the relevant definition, I.D37)
probe each factor.
This chapter identifies *where* those characters live:
the **cyclotomic fields**
ℚ^cyc_τ(ζ_n),
extensions of ℚ_τ generated by roots of unity.
The Galois groups
Gal(ℚ_τ(ζ_n)/ℚ_τ)
≅ (ℤ/nℤ)^×
connect the CRT machinery to field automorphisms,
previewing the Galois theory of Book II.
