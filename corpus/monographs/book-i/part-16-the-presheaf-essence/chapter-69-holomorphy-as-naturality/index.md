Remark [rem:naturality] in Part XIII observed informally
that tower coherence is a naturality condition
on the primorial category.
Part XIV earned the categorical vocabulary —
presheaves, natural transformations, functors.
This chapter closes the loop:
the **Presheaf Characterization Theorem**
(the relevant theorem, I.T40)
proves that τ-holomorphic functions are
*precisely* the natural transformations
from the primorial presheaf to its split-complex extension
that respect the sector decomposition.
No topology.
No geometry.
No analysis.
Pure diagram-chasing on finite cyclic groups.
