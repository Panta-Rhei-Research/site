The unification of the four fundamental forces has been the central aspiration of theoretical physics for a century. Grand Unified Theories embed SU(3)×SU(2)×U(1) into a larger gauge group; string theory adds dimensions; loop quantum gravity quantizes geometry directly. All three approaches seek unification by *adding* structure. Category τ takes the opposite path. Unification is not achieved by adding structure but by recognising that the structure already present in the boundary holonomy algebra H∂[ω] *is* the unification. The four forces are not four separate theories that must be glued together — they are four sectors of a single algebra that was never divided. The single number ι_τ = 2/(π + e) mediates every cross-sector coupling with zero free parameters.

## What this chapter contributes

The chapter proves the **Boundary Completeness Theorem** (τ-effective): all C(4,2) = 6 pairs of primitive sectors {D, A, B, C} satisfy commuting Hartogs squares in H∂[ω]. A commuting Hartogs square for sectors X and Y means that the cross-coupling multiplication map μ_XY is a well-defined morphism of graded algebras and that the diagram relating sector-level multiplication to holonomy-graded multiplication commutes — the interaction between two forces respects the spectral structure of each. The theorem has four parts: each pair has a well-defined cross-coupling; each Hartogs square commutes; the ω-sector mediates all cross-couplings by sitting at the crossing point of 𝕃 = S¹ ∨ S¹; and all six cross-couplings are rational functions of ι_τ with no free parameters. Two supporting lemmas establish: Transporter–Hartogs commutativity (transporter morphisms between sectors are morphisms of filtered modules over the holonomy grading) and Carriers-as-germ-images (every carrier type is a functorial image of the germ functor, making carrier assignments natural in the sector variable). The philosophical lesson is explicit: Maxwell did not unify electricity and magnetism by adding a fifth force — he recognised that the existing phenomena were two aspects of a single entity. Category τ follows Maxwell's template. The unification is not a new theory on top of existing physics but the recognition that the boundary holonomy algebra H∂[ω], derived from seven axioms on five generators, was never divided in the first place.
