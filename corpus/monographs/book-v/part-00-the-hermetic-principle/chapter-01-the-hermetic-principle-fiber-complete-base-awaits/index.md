What remains is the base τ¹, governed by the generators α (Gravity) and π (Weak). The Hermetic Principle, stated formally in this chapter, asserts that fiber plus base is *exact*: every E₁ observable is a readout of H_∂[ω] factoring through either the fiber component, the base component, or one of the six cross-couplings between them. Nothing is left over; nothing is missing. The same master constant ι_τ = 2/(π + e) that fixed the electron mass at the fiber level now controls all macrophysical quantities — G = (c³/ℏ)ι_τ², S_BH = k_B A/(4ι_τ²), and the gravitational closing identity α_G = α¹⁸ · √3 · (1 − (3/π)α).
