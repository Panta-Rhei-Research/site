Headshots are made available in two formats:

- **JPG (sRGB)** — high-quality print master, suitable for editorial layouts and printed handouts.
- **PNG (transparent background)** — suitable for digital layouts, web embeds, and slide decks.

All headshots are sized at 2400 × 3000 (4:5 portrait, the standard editorial aspect ratio) and reproduce cleanly at any size from 200 px web thumbnails to 11×14 print.

## Usage rights

Author headshots are released for editorial use under the following terms:

- **Permitted**: Editorial use accompanying coverage of the Panta Rhei Research Program, the books, the research papers, the formalization library, or related research output. This includes news articles, podcast cover art, conference programs, institutional communications, and academic citations.
- **Permitted with attribution**: Re-use under [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) — share, adapt, with attribution to "Panta Rhei Research Program · panta-rhei.site".
- **Not permitted**: Use in advertising, endorsement, or commercial promotion of unrelated products or services without prior written permission. Use in any context that misrepresents the program's framing or scope.

For specific use-case questions, write to [press@panta-rhei.site](mailto:press@panta-rhei.site) and the authors will reply within 48 hours on weekdays.
