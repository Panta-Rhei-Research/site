Mathematical Content #

Dimensionless Near-Matches #

The τ-framework produces dimensionless couplings from ι_τ alone. Three such couplings can be compared to measured physical constants:

τ-coupling Formula τ-value SI value Deviation
α_EM (8/15)·ι_τ⁴ ~1/137.9 1/137.036 0.6%
sin²θ_W ι_τ(1−ι_τ) ~0.2249 0.23121 2.7%
α_s(M_Z) 2·ι_τ³/(1−ι_τ) ~0.1208 0.1180 2.4%

All three are CONJECTURAL: the numerical near-matches are observed but the exact mechanism connecting ι_τ formulas to measured values requires the full calibration cascade (Parts III–X).
