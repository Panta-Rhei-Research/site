                  structure Tau.BookIV.Arena.MassFiberStiffness :
                  Type

                  [IV.D271] Mass as fiber stiffness: mass = resistance of fiber T² to deformation. Massless modes (radiation) have zero fiber component. Massive modes (defect bundles) have positive fiber stiffness.

                  • carrier : Physics.CarrierType

                    Carrier type determines mass.

                  • is_massive : Bool

                    Fiber or crossing → massive; base → massless.

                  • mass_rule : self.is_massive = (self.carrier != Physics.CarrierType.Base)
