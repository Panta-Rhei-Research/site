Mathematical Content #

III.D57 (Enrichment Functor Enr₀₁): Faithful functor Enr₀₁: Cat_τ(E₀) → Cat_τ(E₁) enriching algebraic tower data with split-complex dynamics and sector structure.

III.D58 (E₁ Mutual Determination Instance): Unified triple (B₁, S₁, I₁) at E₁ with NS regularity, YM gap, and Hodge addressability as three readings.

III.P24 (Three-Reading Equivalence): The E₁ MD instance admits exactly three non-trivial sector-restricted readings (NS, YM, Hodge).
