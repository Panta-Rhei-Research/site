structure Tau.BookVI.Consumer.ConsumerMixer :
Type

[VI.D46] Consumer Mixer on (π', π''): mixed-sector pairing of both fiber generators. The only non-primitive sector. Generator pair: (π', π'') on fiber T² (Book I, Part I). Winding: π₁(T²) ≅ ℤ × ℤ gives two independent winding numbers (Book II, Part II). Both must be nontrivial.

    Generator pair label.

  • is_mixed : Bool

    Mixed sector (not primitive).

  • winding_pi_prime : ℕ

    Winding number on π' (must be ≥ 1).

  • winding_pi_double : ℕ

    Winding number on π'' (must be ≥ 1).

  • pi_prime_nontrivial : self.winding_pi_prime ≥ 1

    π' winding nontrivial.

  • pi_double_nontrivial : self.winding_pi_double ≥ 1

    π'' winding nontrivial.

          Fiber–fiber pairing stable (π',π'' only).
