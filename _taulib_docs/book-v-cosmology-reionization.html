21cm Brightness Temperature [V.D334] #

T₂₁(z) ≈ 27 mK · x_HI · (1 − T_CMB/T_S) · √((1+z)/10 · 0.15/Ω_m) · (ω_b/0.023)

Absorption Trough [V.T271] #

At z ≈ 17 (cosmic dawn), with τ-native Ω_m = 0.315 and ω_b = 0.02238: T₂₁(z=17) ≈ −209 mK (standard ΛCDM evaluated at τ parameters).
