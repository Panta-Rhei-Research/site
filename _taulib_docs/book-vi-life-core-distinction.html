TauLib.BookVI.LifeCore.Distinction #

τ-Distinction: the first of two Life predicates. A predicate D: X → 2_τ satisfying 5 conditions: clopen partition, refinement-coherent, eventually stable, law-stable, H_∂-equivariant.
