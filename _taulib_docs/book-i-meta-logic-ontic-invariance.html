TauLib.MetaLogic.OnticInvariance #

Ontic Identity Invariance theorem, No Identity Decoherence corollary, and the Slippage Breaks Unique Omega theorem.

Mathematical Content #

The Ontic Identity Invariance theorem shows that τ's coherence kernel preserves ontic identity at every construction step. The corollary establishes that diagonal resonance cannot occur. The conditional theorem shows identity slippage prevents unique omega internalization.
