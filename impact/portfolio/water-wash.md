## Overview

Water and WASH is one of the strongest remaining clusters in the τ meta-portfolio: the line from better physical intelligence to public good is extremely short. The portfolio organizes five mission layers under one shared water twin, from source safety and distribution continuity through sanitation and reuse, basin intelligence, and WASH in essential institutions.

## Detail

WHO/UNICEF report 2.1 billion people still lacked safely managed drinking-water services in 2024, 3.4 billion lacked safely managed sanitation, and WHO's burden-of-disease dashboard attributes about 1.4 million deaths and 74 million DALYs in 2019 to unsafe water, sanitation, and hygiene. UN-Water says 42% of household wastewater was not safely treated in 2022. More than 2 billion people live in countries under water stress. Health facilities serving 1.1 billion people lack basic water services, and 447 million children lack basic drinking water at school. The portfolio organizes five linked papers sharing one τ water substrate — source-water hydrology and quality, treatment, distribution hydraulics, wastewater and stormwater flows, groundwater and basin dynamics, and the movement of contaminants and disruptions through those systems.
