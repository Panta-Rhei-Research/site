## Overview

The ocean domain is one of the clearest first-wave τ deployment fields because the same fluid-dynamical core touches global trade, climate mitigation, disaster response, food systems, and ecological stewardship. The portfolio organizes four mission layers under one shared ocean–atmosphere–wave–current twin, starting with maritime logistics and progressing through climate-smart shipping, blue food systems, and ocean stewardship.

## Detail

Maritime transport carries over 80% of world trade by volume. International shipping accounts for roughly 2% of global energy-related CO₂ emissions. Blue foods provide at least 20% of animal protein for 3.2 billion people, and over 600 million people depend on fisheries and aquaculture for livelihoods. NOAA, ECMWF, and Copernicus already operate coupled ocean, wave, and marine forecasting stacks; IMO and green-shipping-corridor coalitions are pushing decarbonization and operational optimization; NOAA and FAO already use ocean intelligence for HAB warning, marine heatwaves, fisheries, spills, and search and rescue. The portfolio organizes four linked papers sharing one substrate — currents, waves, winds, tides, trajectory and drift prediction — and adds commercial, decarbonization, ecological, and stewardship mission layers.
