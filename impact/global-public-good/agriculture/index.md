## Portfolio Focus

Agriculture concerns food-system resilience, crop intelligence, soil-water coupling, pest and disease risk, livestock stress, and climate-adaptive breeding. Its public-good logic is strongest where better physical and biological modeling could reduce avoidable loss before crisis becomes visible.
