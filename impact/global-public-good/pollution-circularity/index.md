## Portfolio Focus

Pollution / Circularity concerns emissions attribution, exposure intelligence, toxic releases, PFAS and heavy metals, waste systems, plastics leakage, and circular reuse. Its conditional value lies in turning diffuse harms into more precise intervention maps.
