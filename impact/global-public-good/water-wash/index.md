## Portfolio Focus

Water / WASH concerns drinking-water early warning, distribution losses, sanitation, reuse, river-basin allocation, drought and flood planning, and vulnerable-settlement continuity. Its conditional value lies in making water-system dynamics more legible before harm arrives.
