## Why this ledger exists

The program should not be judged only by the claims it prefers to highlight. It should also be judged by the questions it agrees to carry.

This ledger is the Program-side mirror of the Results lane. It says what kind of burden is being accepted; the linked Results pages say what the current answer status is.
