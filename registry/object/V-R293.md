summary: The language of dissolution vs. solution is deliberate. A solution to the
  measurement problem would be a mechanism resolving tension within the Dirac-von
  Neumann formulation. A dissolution removes the formulation that produces the tension.
  The five hinges do not solve the hard problems; they dissolve them by replacing
  the framework in which the problems arise.
