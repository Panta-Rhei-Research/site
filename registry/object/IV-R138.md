  because nuclear binding lowers the neutron's effective mass, making Q_beta = (m_n
  - m_p - m_e)c^2 - B_n + B_p negative; when Q_beta < 0 the decay is energetically
  forbidden --- a readout-level phenomenon, the ontic neutron being unchanged.
