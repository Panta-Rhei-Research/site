## Overview

[Book VII]({{ '/publications/books/book-vii/' | relative_url }}) is the third and final self-enrichment of the [coherence kernel]({{ '/framework/mathematics-coherence-kernel/' | relative_url }}). At <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>2</mn></msub></math>, a system [carries and decodes its own code]({{ '/framework/life-life-defined/' | relative_url }}). At <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>3</mn></msub></math>, a system models *the fact that it carries and decodes its own code*. This recursive self-modeling produces four orthogonal **registers** -- four independent modes through which reality can be engaged -- and the **Saturation Theorem** proves that this is the end: there is no <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>4</mn></msub></math>. The enrichment ladder terminates.

## The Core Idea

The four registers (VII.D01-D04) are:

- **Empirical** (<math><msub><mi>S</mi><mi>E</mi></msub></math>): what *is* the case -- factual claims about structure, observation, measurement. The register of [ontology]({{ '/framework/metaphysics-ontology/' | relative_url }}) and [phenomenology]({{ '/framework/metaphysics-phenomenology/' | relative_url }}).

- **Diagrammatic** (<math><msub><mi>S</mi><mi>D</mi></msub></math>): what *follows* from what -- proof, inference, pattern recognition. The register of [aesthetics]({{ '/framework/metaphysics-aesthetics/' | relative_url }}), [language]({{ '/framework/metaphysics-language-meaning/' | relative_url }}), and [logic]({{ '/framework/metaphysics-logic-inference/' | relative_url }}).

- **Practical** (<math><msub><mi>S</mi><mi>P</mi></msub></math>): what *should* be done -- obligation, dignity, fairness. The register of [ethics]({{ '/framework/metaphysics-categorical-ethics/' | relative_url }}) and [social organization]({{ '/framework/metaphysics-societies/' | relative_url }}).

- **Commitment** (<math><msub><mi>S</mi><mi>C</mi></msub></math>): what the agent *constitutes by its own act* -- not propositions to be believed but stances to be taken. The register of [consciousness]({{ '/framework/metaphysics-mind-consciousness/' | relative_url }}) and the [Logos sector]({{ '/framework/metaphysics-logos-sector/' | relative_url }}).

The **Saturation Theorem** (VII.T01) proves <math><mrow><msup><mtext>Enrich</mtext><mn>4</mn></msup><mo>=</mo><msup><mtext>Enrich</mtext><mn>3</mn></msup></mrow></math>: the fourth application of the enrichment functor produces nothing new. Three lemmas establish this: the no-new-lobe lemma (no new lemniscate sectors can form), the no-new-crossing-mediator lemma (no new <math><mi>&omega;</mi></math>-coupling types appear), and carrier closure (the carrier space is already complete). The series has exactly seven books because the [kernel]({{ '/framework/mathematics-canonical-ladder/' | relative_url }}) forces exactly four layers with distribution (3,2,1,1).

## Why This Matters

The four registers organize the entire metaphysical content of Book VII: Parts II-III are empirical, Parts IV-VI are diagrammatic, Parts VII-VIII are practical, and Part IX is commitment. The [Logos sector]({{ '/framework/metaphysics-logos-sector/' | relative_url }}) (Part X) is where diagrammatic and commitment registers coincide -- and Part XI is where the framework [locates its own boundary]({{ '/framework/metaphysics-no-forced-stance/' | relative_url }}). The four-register structure is not a classification imposed on philosophy -- it is derived from the [4+1 sector template]({{ '/framework/mathematics-sector-template/' | relative_url }}) at <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>3</mn></msub></math>.
