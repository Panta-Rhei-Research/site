## The Core Idea

The consumer sector (<math><mrow><mi>&gamma;</mi><mo>,</mo><mi>&eta;</mi></mrow></math> mixed) is the most complex sector by construction: it couples source and closure channels via signature rigidity. The nervous system (VI.D52) instantiates this coupling computationally. Neurons are <math><msup><mi>T</mi><mn>2</mn></msup></math> mode processors; synaptic connections are boundary-character transitions; the brain is a network of such transitions that performs address resolution in the [ABCD chart]({{ '/framework/mathematics-abcd-chart/' | relative_url }}) at biological scale.

Sleep (VI.D68) is reinterpreted as temporal lemniscate navigation: the sleep-wake cycle corresponds to the system traversing both lobes of the [lemniscate]({{ '/framework/mathematics-prime-polarity/' | relative_url }}) boundary, consolidating self-model updates that occurred during the waking lobe. Learning is boundary-character update -- the system modifies its internal character assignments in response to environmental signals.

**Consciousness** is defined structurally (not phenomenologically -- that is [Book VII]({{ '/publications/books/book-vii/' | relative_url }})): it is the consumer sector's [SelfDesc]({{ '/framework/life-life-defined/' | relative_url }}) applied to its own modeling apparatus. The self-model includes a model of itself-as-modeler. This recursive self-reference is what bridges <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>2</mn></msub></math> to <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>3</mn></msub></math>: when the self-model becomes the object of its own descriptive capacity, the system crosses into the metaphysical layer.

The <math><mi>&omega;</mi></math>-germ question -- whether this structural self-reference resolves into a complete account of subjective experience -- cannot be answered diagrammatically within <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>2</mn></msub></math>. This is the principled science-faith boundary. Six export contracts close [Book VI]({{ '/publications/books/book-vi/' | relative_url }}); Book VII opens at consciousness.
