## Overview

The most philosophically striking result in [Book V]({{ '/publications/books/book-v/' | relative_url }}): the orthodox second law is *inverted*. The arrow of time does not point toward chaos -- it points toward **order**. Total entropy still increases (as the orthodox law demands), but when decomposed into its structural components, the physically relevant component -- *defect entropy* -- decreases monotonically to zero. The universe is not running down. It is running *up*.

## The Core Idea

The framework decomposes total entropy into two components (V.D39):

Heat is reinterpreted as electromagnetic B-sector transport: radiation, conduction, and convection are all B-sector (EM) mechanisms, not kinetic billiards. This reinterpretation eliminates the need for a cosmological constant: apparent cosmic acceleration is a [readout artifact]({{ '/framework/physics-no-dark-energy/' | relative_url }}) of the entropy splitting, not a mysterious energy component.

## Why This Matters

The thermodynamic inversion connects to the [dark sector resolution]({{ '/framework/physics-no-dark-matter/' | relative_url }}): if the universe is moving toward order rather than chaos, the cosmological constant is unnecessary and dark energy dissolves as a readout artifact. It also sets up the [CMB pipeline]({{ '/framework/physics-cmb-pipeline/' | relative_url }}): the arrow of time determines the initial conditions for the cosmic microwave background.
