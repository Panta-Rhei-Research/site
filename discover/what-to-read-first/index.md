## If Unsure

Use [Start Here]({{ '/discover/start-here/' | relative_url }}) first. It is the safest route for a new visitor who does not yet know which register matters most.

If you want the site's deepest structure, choose the Research architecture path. If you want the fastest sense of substance, choose Key Results first.
