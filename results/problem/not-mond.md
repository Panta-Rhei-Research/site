## Overview

MOND (Modified Newtonian Dynamics) modifies gravity at low accelerations to explain galaxy rotation curves. The τ-framework derives flat rotation curves from the boundary reading without modifying gravity — the framework is not MOND.

## Result Statement

τ-framework is not MOND: flat rotation curves arise from boundary reading, not modified gravity. Contradicts MOND. Status: Contradicted.
