## Overview

The translation functor from Category τ to ZFC (Zermelo-Fraenkel set theory with Choice) is the bridge that would allow τ-results to be stated in conventional mathematical language. The functor exists structurally but its full construction is incomplete.

## Result Statement

Translation functor τ → ZFC is partially constructed. Number theory and algebra translate cleanly; full set-theoretic bridge is incomplete. Status: Partial.
