## Overview

The ABC Conjecture, proposed by Masser and Oesterlé in 1985, constrains the relationship between the additive and multiplicative structure of integers. Mochizuki's claimed proof (2012) via Inter-Universal Teichmüller Theory remains contested.

ABC Conjecture is structurally adjacent to the hyperfactorization theorem. No derivation yet. Status: Not Addressed.
