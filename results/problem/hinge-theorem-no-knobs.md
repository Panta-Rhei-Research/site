## Overview

The No Knobs Theorem (III.T42) proves that all 10 inter-sector couplings are rational functions of ι<sub>τ</sub> = 2/(π+e) — zero free continuous parameters. Together these two theorems are the structural explanation for why physics books exist and why their predictions are testable without tuning.

This makes all predictions in Books IV–V testable and falsifiable from a single master constant.
