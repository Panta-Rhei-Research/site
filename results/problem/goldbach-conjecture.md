## Overview

Goldbach's Conjecture (1742) — every even integer greater than 2 is the sum of two primes — remains one of the oldest unsolved problems in number theory. The τ-framework has structural tools (prime polarity, bipolar decomposition) that could potentially address it, but no derivation has been completed.
