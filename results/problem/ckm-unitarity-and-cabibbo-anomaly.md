## Overview

The Cabibbo Anomaly -- apparent unitarity violation in the first row of the CKM quark mixing matrix -- has attracted attention as a possible signal of physics beyond the Standard Model. The <math><mi>&tau;</mi></math>-framework derives the CKM matrix from the [three-generation]({{ '/corpus/monographs/' | relative_url }}) structure of <math><mrow><msub><mi>&pi;</mi><mn>1</mn></msub><mo>(</mo><msup><mi>&tau;</mi><mn>3</mn></msup><mo>)</mo></mrow></math>, but the unitarity constraint requires further work.

The mixing angles are derived from the [lemniscate]({{ '/corpus/monographs/' | relative_url }}) character overlap between generation modes.
