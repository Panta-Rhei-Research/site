## Overview

Consciousness is not a single-layer phenomenon. It spans three enrichment layers in the framework: <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>1</mn></msub></math> (physical neural correlates in the [microcosm]({{ '/corpus/monographs/' | relative_url }})), <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>2</mn></msub></math> (biological self-model in the [consumer sector]({{ '/corpus/monographs/' | relative_url }})), and <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>3</mn></msub></math> (phenomenal experience and the [global section]({{ '/corpus/monographs/' | relative_url }}) account). Each layer's account is individually developed, but the full cross-layer integration -- how physical neural activity (<math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>1</mn></msub></math>) gives rise to biological self-modeling (<math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>2</mn></msub></math>) which in turn produces phenomenal experience (<math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>3</mn></msub></math>) -- is not yet complete.

## Detail

At <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>1</mn></msub></math>: neurons are <math><msup><mi>T</mi><mn>2</mn></msup></math> mode processors (Book IV). At <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>2</mn></msub></math>: consciousness is recursive SelfDesc -- the self-model models itself (Book VI). At <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>3</mn></msub></math>: consciousness is a global section (VII.T41), free will is genuine branching (VII.T42-T43), and the [Hard Problem]({{ '/results/problem/hard-problem-of-consciousness/' | relative_url }}) is addressed structurally. The gap is in the *transitions*: how does the enrichment functor map neural <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>1</mn></msub></math> structure to biological <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>2</mn></msub></math> self-modeling, and how does <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>2</mn></msub></math> self-modeling produce <math><msub><mi>E</mi><mn>3</mn></msub></math> phenomenality? The [No Forced Stance]({{ '/corpus/monographs/' | relative_url }}) theorem (VII.T47) suggests the final transition may be principally non-derivable.

## Result Statement

Each layer's account is developed; cross-layer transition mechanisms are incomplete. Status: **Partial** *(tau-effective at each layer; conjectural for the transitions)*.
