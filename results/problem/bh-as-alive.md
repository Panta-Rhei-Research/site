## Overview

Are black holes alive? The τ-framework provides a precise answer: black holes satisfy some but not all hallmarks of life. They have boundary (event horizon) and persistence but lack autonomous Poincaré circulation.

## Result Statement

Black holes are not alive in the τ-sense: they lack autonomous Poincaré circulation despite having boundary and persistence. Status: Internally addressed (consequence).
