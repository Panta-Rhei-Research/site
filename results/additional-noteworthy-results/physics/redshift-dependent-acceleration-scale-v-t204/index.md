## Result summary

Redshift-Dependent Acceleration Scale: a_0(z) = c*H(z)*iota/2, where H(z) = H_0*sqrt(Omega_m*(1+z)^3+Omega_Lambda). At z=2: a_0 = 3.03*a_0(0).
