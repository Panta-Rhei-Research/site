Unlike physics's linear cascade, the life calibration cascade is a **multi-branch tree** rooted at one structural anchor: the **Kinetic Pseudoscalar Channel** (`LG-Y02`, registry `VI.L18`).

The K_χ channel breaks parity at the weak-sector level and amplifies the seed (~10⁻¹⁷ relative chirality bias) into biological-scale homochirality (ee ≈ 1.0). Every life-sector empirical correlate inherits its observable signature from this anchor.

## The five branches

### Chirality branch (the canonical anchor branch)
- **Anchor:** `LG-Y02` K_χ channel (`VI.L18`)
- **Outputs:** L-amino-acid dominance in proteins (ee ≈ 1.0); D-sugar dominance in nucleic acids
- **Observation:** polarimetry, circular dichroism, chiral chromatography, CISS experiments (Paltiel et al. 2026-04-22)
- **Glossary entries:** `LG-S01-cell`, `LG-S02..S15` (15 structures)

### Energy branch
- **Branches from:** K_χ → stereospecific enzyme binding
- **Outputs:** ATP/ADP cycle at 10:1 ratio; respiration; photosynthesis; electron transport
- **Observation:** ³¹P NMR; luciferase assay; Seahorse extracellular flux
- **Glossary entries:** `LG-E01-atp` through `LG-E09` (9 entries)

### Information branch
- **Branches from:** K_χ → DNA D-ribose chirality preserved
- **Outputs:** Genetic code as BSD motivic structure; epigenetic state propagation
- **Observation:** sequencing, ribosome profiling, epigenetic mark assays
- **Glossary entries:** `LG-I01..LG-I11` (11 entries)

### Temporal branch
- **Branches from:** K_χ → asymmetric circadian protein folding
- **Outputs:** Circadian rhythm; cell cycle; generation time
- **Observation:** transcriptomics over time; behavioural assays
- **Glossary entries:** `LG-T01..LG-T09` (9 entries)

### Phenomenal branch
- **Branches from:** K_χ → neural-defect asymmetry
- **Outputs:** sleep; consciousness; cognition; sensory experience
- **Observation:** fMRI, EEG, behavioural psychophysics
- **Glossary entries:** `LG-M01..LG-M09` (9 entries)
