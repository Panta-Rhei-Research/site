## What is the τ-framework saying about life?

In plain language: the τ-framework claims that the universal features of life on Earth — the dominance of L-amino acids and D-sugars (homochirality), the universality of ATP as energy currency, the genetic code's specific codon→amino-acid map, the prevalence of circadian timing — are not historical accidents but **structural consequences** of how the weak-sector K_χ channel propagates a tiny chirality seed (~10⁻¹⁷) up through every living scale.

This makes life chemistry-independent: any planet with K_χ-coupled chemistry should evolve the same handedness. It also makes ATP's role and the genetic code's structure inevitable — not just possible, but predicted from the structure of the τ kernel.

The 78-entry [Life Glossary]({{ '/results/life/glossary/' | relative_url }}) carries an empirical correlate for every concept: a biomarker, a measurable range, an observation method. Every chirality-sensitive correlate anchors to LG-Y02 via the K_χ channel.
